## Player & Staff Information Policy

This site publishes information about players, staff, and other individuals associated with Atlanta United FC and related organizations. Our goal is to celebrate, document, and inform supporters about the team and its history.

### What We Publish

The information we publish may include:

- Player and staff names
- Biographical details such as nationality or birthdate
- Match appearances, statistics, and achievements
- Publicly known career history (clubs, transfers, awards, etc.)
- Social media handles that are already public

### How Information Is Obtained

Content displayed on this site is based on **publicly visible** materials related to professional sports,
including match data, team announcements, media reporting, and public social media activity.
We make a good-faith effort to publish only information relevant to professional careers.

This includes, but is not limited to:
- Team, league, or media outlets
- Photography credited to its original source where possible
- Public player or staff social media accounts
- Statistics and career data services
- Sports journalism, broadcasts, and official match documents

If you believe content has been shared in a way that creates a privacy, rights, or safety concern,
please contact us so we can review and address the issue promptly.

---

### Respect & Corrections

We aim to ensure everything presented is accurate and respectful.  
If you are a player, staff member, family representative, team representative, authorized agent, media rights holder, or anyone with a legitimate concern and would like to:

- Correct any published information  
- Remove or minimize certain personal details  
- Stop being tagged or referenced in social posts  
- Remove or replace photos where rights are unclear or permissions change  

You can contact us and we will review and respond promptly.

### No User Tracking or User Data Collection

We do **not** track or profile website visitors.  
We do **not** collect personal information from users unless you voluntarily contact us.

We do not sell, rent, or share any contact details with third parties.
